What We Do

Focused on mobile app development since 2013. Over the years, we've been developing our own mobile app platform and CMS. Working with media companies and sports organizations across four continents, helping them shape their digital strategy through flexible, scalable solutions. Alongside our mobile tech, we've also started building AI-powered tools like Litteraworks, and we're spending more and more time figuring out how AI can improve the products we already offer.

How We Work

We don't start from scratch with every new partner. Instead, we use our modular app platform as a base, which means development is faster, more affordable, and still highly customizable. Each app feels tailor-made, but without the huge cost or time investment. We adapt the design, features, and functionality to match the needs of each partner. Our product is always evolving—we never really see it as "finished." New ideas are always in the pipeline, and we love working closely with our partners to test and launch fresh features. AI plays a big part in this, helping us automate and personalize content in smarter ways.

Where We Operate

We've been building things from Belgrade for over a decade, but our reach is global. Today, we collaborate with media and sports partners across four continents. We stay small, curious, and focused—always exploring new ideas, but staying grounded in strong, long-term partnerships.
